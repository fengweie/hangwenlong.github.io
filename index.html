Biography

I am a Assistant Professor in Nanjing Tech University. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree at Jiangnan University, Wuxi, China, in 2017. I am currently an assistant professor in the School of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing Tech University, Nanjing, China. My current research interests include transfer learning, deep learning and biomedical signal processing.

My research lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence and medical image analysis. I am dedicated to designing data-efficient learning methods for biomedical image analysis. I also have expertise in deep learning for EEG. Recently, I am working on new learning algorithms while minimizing human supervision (self-supervised, semi-supervised) and medical image analysis.

If you would like to work with me, feel free to send me an email. Remote collaboration is also welcome!
